
Bubble Shooter game

- Easter eggs keep falling from the sky 
- Shoot bubbles at the eggs to secure them
- If bubble shot correctly hits the egg , it secures the egg and increases your score
- If bubble does not hit anything , it explodes into particles
- If egg falling doesn't get covered in bubble , it crashes

- Obstacles
    - If bubble hits a bomb , it explodes violently

- Power Ups
